Jesse Wellens is an American YouTube sensation best known for his PrankvsPrank channel where you can watch videos of him pulling pranks on others. Created in 2009, the channel now has more than 10 million subscribers. The Wellens Channel was named YouTube’s Best Prankster in 2013 by USA Today. In 2015, he received the Streamy Award for Best Joke Show and the following year a Shorty Award for Best YouTube Ensemble.

Jesse Wellens Facts / Age

Jesse Michael Wellens was born on September 25, 1982, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to Stella and Steve Wellens. He is the middle child in the family and has a sibling named Candice Wellens.

Wellens was one of those kids who liked to bully other kids at school. This saw him get kicked out of high school on several occasions, but he managed to make it to graduation. Even though Jesse may seem insane, he served in the army… For a year he was posted to Korea. While in the U.S. Army, Jesse was in a marriage that ended almost immediately.

After his divorce, Jesse would meet Jennifer “Jeana” Smith through a mutual friend named Faith. They exchanged phone numbers but didn’t start a relationship until they met weeks later. They started dating in 2005.

Jesse and Jeana in 2009 started a YouTube channel together. It all started after Jesse posted a clip of Jeana doing the cinnamon challenge. This then resulted in a series of prank videos featuring one prank on the other and how they planned their pranks.

Their sense of humor and quirkiness have garnered millions of subscribers, making them one of YouTube’s friendliest couples. Soon they started taking other gigs, like when they both appeared in two episodes of the Epic Rap Battles of History web series.

Mom – Stella Wellens

Jesse’s mother, Stella Wellens, died in December 2015. She had suffered from depression for over ten years and used drugs while on drugs. Stella was also suicidal and had been put into a coma after an unsuccessful suicide attempt.

A few weeks before his death, Jesse’s mother had been declared missing. Jesse expressed his fears that his mother was suicidal since his grandmother had just passed away a few weeks earlier.

Jesse was right about his fears… Local authorities would later discover Stella’s body in her car parked in a business parking lot. She was laid to rest later in December 2015.
