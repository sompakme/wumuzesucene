G Lydian Cheat Sheet

Publish date: 2024-07-09

The key of G Lydian has a key signature of 2 sharps (F#, and C#). The G Lydian scale has the notes G, A, B, C#, D, E, and F#. It is the 5th most popular key among Lydian keys and the 62nd most popular among all keys. The G Lydian scale is similar to the G Major scale except that its 4th note (C♯) is a half step higher. Music written in Lydian often emphasizes this difference by creating melodies that feature this note.
