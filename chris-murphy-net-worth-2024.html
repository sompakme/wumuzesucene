Who is Chris Murphy?

Chris Murphy is an American politician and lawyer who has been serving as the junior United States Senator from Connecticut since 2013. A member of the Democratic Party, Murphy has been active in politics for several years, with his career spanning roles in the Connecticut General Assembly, the U.S. House of Representatives, and now the U.S. Senate.

Early Life and Career

Chris Murphy was born on August 3, 1973, in White Plains, New York, and grew up in Wethersfield, Connecticut. He attended Williams College and later graduated from the University of Connecticut School of Law. Murphy’s political career began early when he was elected to the Connecticut General Assembly in 1998, serving in the House of Representatives and later in the State Senate.

Transition to National Politics
