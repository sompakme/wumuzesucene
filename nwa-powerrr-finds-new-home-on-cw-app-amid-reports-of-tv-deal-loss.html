NWA Powerrr Finds New Home on CW App Amid Reports of TV Deal Loss

Publish date: 2024-09-14

The NWA got a lot of attention unexpected attention when James Mitchell was part of a cocaine-filled angle on one of their pay-per-view events recently. This did not turn out the way NWA had hoped as it only gave the company a lot of bad publicity, with reports noting the whole angle cost NWA a deal with The CW. That said, NWA Powerrr has now found a new home on the CW app amid reports of them losing the TV deal.

It was previously reported that the NWA may have tainted their deal with the CW over that cocaine spot at the NWA’s recent PPV event, Samhain. It was then announced that the CW would become NXT’s home from next year, due to which Billy Cogan got criticized by fans and pro wrestlers alike, especially Eric Bischoff.

Interestingly enough, if fans browse the CW app, they will find that NWA Powerrr is currently available there. For the time being, only eight episodes of the series’ 15th season are available for streaming, listed in the “Recently Added” section.

That said, the NWA is expected to get roster as well as production cuts in the future. Billy Corgan doubled down on the NWA landing TV deal with top 20 network recently as well. Regardless, we’ll have to wait and see what will become of NWA in the end.
