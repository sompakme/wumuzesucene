CAA Ordered to Evacuate After Bomb Squad Call

Publish date: 2024-09-15

A bomb squad was called to CAA headquarters in Century City on Wednesday. 

The agency received instructions to evacuate, a receptionist told The Hollywood Reporter.

At 1:30 pm, authorities were informed by a call from Deutsche Bank that there was a suspicious package delivered by FedEx on the ninth floor of the building, said Los Angeles Police Department spokesperson Nuria Vanegas. The package was on the opposite side of the building from CAA.

The entire building, located at 2000 Avenue of the Stars, was evacuated. The immediate area surrounding the building was also closed off. 

In addition to CAA, the building houses several Hollywood law firms and businesses as well as a popular screening room.
