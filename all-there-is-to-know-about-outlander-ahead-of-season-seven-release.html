All there is to know about Outlander ahead of season seven release

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Sam Heughan and Caitriona Balfe will soon be back on our screen

    The Droughtlander is nearly over! Brand new episodes of the historical series starring Sam Heughan and Caitriona Balfe are on the way and fans are thrilled about having Jamie and Claire Fraser back on their screens once again.  

    From what we know so far, the seventh season of is going to be brilliant. But with the new episodes comes the bittersweet news that fans may have to wait a little longer for the full season. Here's all you need to know about the upcoming release from the plot, release dates and more.

    To get you in the mood, check out the video below of Sam and Caitriona testing their knowledge on the popular series.

    When is Outlander season seven being released?

    fans were thrilled when the show recently announced the air date for new episodes. A tweet from the drama's official account revealed: "Summer on Fraser's Ridge sounds lovely, don't you think?

    "That's why I'm excited to announce that #Outlander Season 7 officially premieres JUNE 16! The sixteen-episode season will be split into two parts with the first half premiering June 16 and the second half airing in 2024." Mark your diaries, folks!

    What will Outlander season seven be about?

    Some clues about what's in store for Outlander season seven have been revealed – and we cannot wait.

    HELLO! previously reported that season seven will pick up from the events of season six, which saw Claire on trial for the murder of Malva Christie while Jamie is en route to save her alongside Young Ian and John Quincy Myers after being rescued from Richard Brown and his men.

    A teaser trailer released at the end of 2022 also hinted at the plot. The teaser feature a number of poignant moments, including Brianna giving birth to her second child, and what appears to be Claire facing the noose – but will Malva Christie's murderer finally be revealed? Bring it on.

    Who is starring in Outlander season seven?

    Of course, wouldn't be without its leading stars Sam Heughan and Caitriona Balfe who will be back to reprise their roles as Jamie and Claire Fraser. Also appearing in the new episodes are favourites such as Richard Ranking, Sophie Skelton, John Bell, Lauren Lyle, Mark Lewis Jones, and more.

    What is the future of Outlander beyond season seven?

    Although there's plenty of content on the horizon thanks to the extended run of season seven, fans will be sad to learn that the programme announced that the eighth season will be the show's last. 

    But, fans need not worry too much because there is a prequel in the works! Producers of the show have greenlit the spin-off show, which will follow Jamie's parents, Brian and Ellen.
