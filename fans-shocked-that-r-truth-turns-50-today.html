Fans Shocked That R-Truth Turns 50 Today

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Ron Killings, also known as R-Truth, has been a mainstay in WWE for years. Truth is known to be very easy to work with and is willing to do almost anything he’s asked. It’s a rare charisma that makes it possible for a superstar to outlast so many others who came before them.

Killings is a former NWA World’s Heavyweight Champion. As R-Truth, he most recently had a wild run as 24/7 Champion. Most people don’t realize how long Truth has been around.

Today marks R-Truth’s 50th birthday. A simple announcement of the occasion on Twitter had fans completely baffled. Nobody realized how old the still-youthful star really was.

“found r truth 50 years old how is 50 when he looks 25”

“R Truth is 50????????????????”

“I can’t believe R Truth is 50 years old”

“I refuse to believe R Truth is 50 years old, he hasn’t aged one bit.”

Nobody can believe R-Truth is 50 years old today. It’s the truth, though. Happy birthday to R-Truth, one of WWE’s most versatile and popular wrestlers!
