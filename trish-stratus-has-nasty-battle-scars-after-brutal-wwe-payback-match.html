Trish Stratus Has Nasty Battle Scars After Brutal WWE Payback Match

Publish date: 2024-09-10

WWE Hall of Famer Trish Stratus has accomplished a lot in the business and paved the way for future generations of women’s wrestlers. She has been back in the fold for a while now and it appears she suffered some nasty battle scars after his brutal steel cage match at Payback.

Trish Stratus and Becky Lynch’s feud culminated in a steel cage match at the WWE Payback Premium Live Event. It featured a lot of huge bumps taken by Stratus and it was truly a physical affair. In the end, Becky Lynch put down Stratus with an avalanche Manhandle slam for the win.

Following her loss, Trish Stratus could be seen making her way to the back while displaying nasty battle scars on her head following the match. She would get a standing ovation from the crowd as she took a bow before heading to the back.

Zoey Stark also turned on Trish Stratus following her loss, so it remains to be seen whether WWE will have anything planned for Stratus and Stark after what transpired at the event.
