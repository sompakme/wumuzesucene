Jennifer Lopez has just shared her anti-cellulite secret

Publish date: 2024-08-30

JLo Body has finally launched two new products…

    Jennifer Lopez stepped into the position of ‘Jenny from the body care block’ this year, as she launched her first body products for JLo beauty – the incredibly successful brand she began in January 2021.

    Following the triumph of her beauty products, the All I Had singer then announced her venture into bodycare with JLo Body. Its first (and only product until now) was the Firm + Flaunt Targeted Booty Balm. When launching the body range, JLo posted in her Instagram saying: “For me, it's all about feeling confident in your skin, no matter what. That's why working with the best chemists in top labs to bring you a formula that could do just that and deliver real results was so important to me. This is seriously sexy science. @JLoBeauty”. Unsurprisingly, this product also sold out immediately.

    Now, Mrs Affleck has given us all a belated Christmas treat, by announcing the expansion of JLo Body on Instagram last night, with two new products – a body serum and a body cream. She said:

    “Today, I’m so proud to introduce the two newest additions to your @JLoBeauty body care routine —

    ✨ Tighten + Tease Ultimate Body Serum: a highly efficacious body serum that gently resurfaces for a more radiant, brighter-looking appearance. I use this daily on my legs, arms, chest and stomach area.

    ✨ Smooth + Seduce Hydrating Body Cream: high-performance formula proven to provide immediate hydration while helping reduce the appearance of skin dimpling and loss of firmness for a more contoured silhouette. This cream is ultra-luxe and so hydrating that I use it twice a day to show my body some love.

    This Body Serum and Body Cream boost my all over glow AND my confidence.”
