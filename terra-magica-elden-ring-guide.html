Terra Magica - Elden Ring Guide

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Terra Magica is an enhancing Glintstone Sorcery in Elden Ring, found after clearing the Academy Crystal Cave in North Liurnia.

advertisement

One of the glintstone sorceries of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.Draws an academy sigil upon the ground, raising the magic strength of those within. Once, the sigil would be cast from the highest belfry of the academy, covering the entire institution's grounds. This spell allows such vivid experience of spell—casting success, it turns many a fledgling into a true sorcerer in a flash of newfound understanding.

Where to Get Terra Magica

Terra Magica is obtained by clearing the Academy Crystal Cave located under the Academy of Raya Lucaria sealed by an imp gate on the western base of the academy. After defeating the bosses, take the elevator past them to reach a tower within Raya Lucaria, where you'll find the spell at the top of a tower.

Terra Magica Stats and Effects

  • Requirements: 20 Intelligence
  • Cost: 20 FP, 12 Stamina
  • Memory Slots Used: 1

Terra Magica buffs magic strength while you stand on the glyph, making it a fantastic spell to use in tandem with Comet Azur. Because it is part of the Comet Azur 1-shot build, it makes our “Best Sorceries” list
