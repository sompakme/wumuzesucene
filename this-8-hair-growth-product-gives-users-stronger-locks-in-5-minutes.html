This $8 Hair Growth Product Gives Users Stronger Locks in 5 Minutes

Publish date: 2024-09-20

If you ask me, the best spring accessory is a full and bouncy head of hair. And since many of us weren’t blessed with naturally thick locks, we must rely on nourishing products to give our strands some extra oomph. Just in time for warmer weather, Amazon shoppers put me on to the Soapbox Oh My Growth Hair Growth Treatment, which happens to be on sale for just $8. 

In addition to its budget-friendly price tag, the lengthening product stands out because of its all-star ingredients. The formula is made with biotin to thicken strands, vitamin A to improve scalp health, vitamin C to protect the skin and hair from environmental damage, vegan collagen to strengthen hair follicles, and an omega-3 blend to increase shine. It’s also free of silicones and parabens and safe to use on color-treated hair, per the brand. 

For the best results, Soapbox recommends using the treatment three to five times a week. All you have to do is massage a few drops into freshly shampooed hair, let it sit for five minutes, and rinse it out.
