My Testimony Is Huge: Actress Didi Ekanem Moves Into New Home 2 Weeks After Losing House, Properties

Publish date: 2024-08-06
  • Nollywood actress, Didi Ekanem, has shared her testimony on social media after she lost all her life’s properties in a house fire
  • Just two weeks after the film star’s Lagos mansion was razed, she has now moved to a spacious new home
  • Ekanem took to social media to share the good news and celebrate as she posted a video of her new space

Popular Nollywood actress, Didi Ekanem, has now moved into a new home just weeks after her Lagos mansion was razed by fire.

Taking to her Instagram page, the movie star shared the good news of her new abode with fans on social media.

Ekanem referred to the move as a huge testimony as she recounted how God did it for her.

According to her, she felt lost and confused at first after the fire destroyed her home and properties. However, she started trusting and believing in God.

The actress then added that where God took her to was bigger than what she lost because it was better than where He brought her from.

Didi Ekanem concluded her post by telling fans to put their faith and trust in God and He will do great things for them.

Part of her post reads:

“When the fire incident happened, at first I was confused and lost … then I started trusting and believing in God to get me though it without knowing that where God is taking me to is way better than where He brought me from. Without knowing also that what He was about to give me is bigger than what He took away from me.Exercise your faith and trust in God and see what God will do in your life.”
