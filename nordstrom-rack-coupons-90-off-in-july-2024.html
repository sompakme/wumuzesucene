FAQs

Is there a Nordstrom Rack free shipping code?

You don't need a Nordstrom Rack coupon to save on shipping. Most orders of $89 and over will become eligible for their free standard shipping option. Please note that Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, and Colorado incur surcharges and are not eligible for free shipping.  

How can I shop the Nordstrom Rack sale?

Simply head to the ‘Clearance’ page to shop the sale all year long where items have been reduced by up to 80% in the past. Nordstrom Rack also runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, and New Year’s.

Can I get a Nordstrom Rack email sign up discount?

First-order coupons have been available in the past, however, it isn't available currently. Should this change, we will update this page with all the relevant information so can continue to save. 

Can I get a Nordstrom Rack student discount?

Unfortunately, Nordstrom does not have any student discount, as of writing. However, you can still save on your clothing by shopping with one of our Nordstrom Rack promo codes or by checking the sale for any discounts. Also, we'll be sure to let you know if a Nordstrom Rack student discount code becomes available anytime soon. 

Does Nordstrom Rack price match?

As it already has competitive prices, Nordstrom Rack does not offer any price matching or adjustments. It’s worth signing up for the Nordstrom newsletter to be kept updated should they choose to give price matching at any time.  

What is the Nordstrom Rack return policy?

Nordstrom Rack accepts returns made after 30 days for in-store purchases and up to 40 days after the purchase date for online purchases. Please note that all items must be returned in their original, unworn condition, with no signs of wear or use, and with original tags and packaging intact.

How to use your Nordstrom Rack coupon code

  • Browse the Marie Claire website for your ideal Nordstrom Rack promo code.
  • Click the link to open Nordstrom Rack in a new browser window.
  • Add your chosen items to your basket.
  • Click the bag icon at the top right corner to checkout.
  • Click ‘promotions’ on the checkout page and the promo box will appear.
  • Copy and paste your chosen Nordstrom Rack coupon code into the promo box.
  • Click ‘Apply’ and enjoy your savings!
