Harry Styles Spotted Driving Convertible and Speedboat in Italy

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Zayn Malik might be called dad now, but fellow One Direction alum Harry Styles is daddy.

You know what I mean—and if you don't, just look at the new photos of Styles scooting his way through narrow Italian streets in the driver's seat of a sleek silver convertible sports car. With his chestnut locks in a muss and his chiseled face set into a grimace, there's little room to wonder why Styles continues to be the subject of many a Wattpad fan fiction. Other photos, published by TMZ, capture the singer zipping down the Amalfi Coast in a speedboat, á la James Bond.

Per Capital FM, fans think Styles is in the process of shooting a new music video for "Golden," one of the singles off his sophomore album, Fine Line. In videos captured by fans, Styles runs down a street wearing a loose white button-up and shorts, seemingly his Italian uniform of choice.

Whatever Styles is doing with those land and water toys, there's no doubt he has spent much of the last few months in Italy. He grew out a furry mustache, for instance, while quarantining with friends and buying fancy olive oil in the country. Then, he cracked his fan base in half when he naturally didn't shave it off.

When not growing facial hair, Styles has been busy growing in other ways. The Stevie Nicks surrogate released a sunshine-laced music video for "Watermelon Sugar" earlier this past summer and has also secured a starring role in Olivia Wilde's upcoming Don't Worry Darling. Most recently, reports revealed that he is in talks to star in My Policeman, a queer love story set in the 1950s and based on the book by Bethan Roberts.
