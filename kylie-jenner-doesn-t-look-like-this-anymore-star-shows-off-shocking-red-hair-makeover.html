Kylie Jenner doesn't look like this anymore! Star shows off shocking red hair makeover

Publish date: 2024-07-19

She's no stranger to daring makeovers and Kylie Jenner showed off one of her most shocking looks yet ahead of her 19th birthday this week. Kim Kardashian's youngest sister took to social media to share snaps of her tresses dyed red and styled into cornrows.     

Kylie – who boasts more than 70million followers on Instagram – appeared to be very happy with the result, as she captioned the photo with a simple love heart emoji. The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star shared a similar post on Snapchat and wrote: "Fire red for 19."

Kylie Jenner dyed her hair shocking red ahead of her 19th birthday

In the pictures cosmetics entrepreneur Kylie has a large red lipstick kiss on her cheek, undoubtedly created with her hugely successful Lip Kit products.

While some fans have suggested Kylie's new colour is a really good Photoshop job, the Snapchat video seems to prove the teen has definitely taken the plunge.

Boyfriend Tyga treated Kylie to a brand new car

And it seems Kylie has a lot to smile about after rapper boyfriend Tyga treated her to a brand new car. He forked out more than £150,000 on a Mercedes-Benz Maybach for his love, after previously gifting her with a Ferrari when she turned 18.

Of course Kylie couldn't resist showing off her new wheels and posted a short clip on Instagram, featuring the sports car topped with a cute red ribbon. She wrote: "Thank you baby @kinggoldchains."

Kylie's Snapchat followers also got a preview of her generous birthday gift, and added alongside one snap: "Happy early birthday to me. Yay! I love you T. Almost too boss for me."
