Megan Thee Stallion Shows Off Her Toned Tummy In &quot;I Love To Make Boys Cry&quot; Crop Top

Publish date: 2024-08-23

She's back. On Friday (April 7), Megan Thee Stallion's Instagram feed lit up with yet another iconic photo dump, this one pulling focus to her toned physique in a pair of unbuttoned light-wash jeans. The top half of her look consisted of a cropped white tee, featuring the words "I ❤️ To Make Boys Cry" markedly written across her chest. As always, the rap diva accessorized with plenty of flashy pieces, including a selection of bracelets and a large cross chain. Since making her comeback last month, Thee Stallion has been sporting her hair in a magnificent afro – a trend that her friends and fans have been loving.

The Houston native's first two images show her posing sultrily in front of a wall. While she maintains a serious expression in the first snapshot, we later see her famous tongue make an appearance as she raises her hands to show off her necklace for the camera. In Thee Stallion's final two photos, she appears to be having the time of her life while riding around in a car with friends. Her goofy side is clearly on display in images three and four, leaving Thee Hotties feeling happy to see Megan outside and smiling once again.

Megan Thee Stallion's Major Serve

She's in the midst of a huge comeback right now, but as you may recall, the 28-year-old spent the first few months of the year laying low and focusing on herself. Though she came out victorious in Tory Lanez's shooting trial in late 2022, Thee Stallion still required time to recover from the media firestorm that ensued as a result of her seeking justice. As he remains behind bars, the Canadian's legal team is seeking a re-trial, which the prosecution recently argued "lacks substance" on his part.

Aside from the legal woes, Megan Thee Stallion is shaping up to have an incredible 2023. Not only did she crush her first live performance of the year in her hometown last weekend, but the "Big Ole Freak" hitmaker has also been named as a headliner at several upcoming festivals.
