CJ Perrys Stunning Photo Drop Builds Anticipation for AEW Collision Appearance

Publish date: 2024-09-11

AEW All Out was a big night for the company, as it was the first event following CM Punk’s firing. It featured a lot of surprises, which included CJ Perry’s debut at the event. Since then, Perry has made another appearance on last week’s Collision. Now it appears Perry hyped up her appearance on this week’s Collision with a photo drop.

Following her WWE release back in 2021, fans have always wondered if she would join her husband Miro in AEW. After a long wait, CJ Perry made her debut at AEW All Out and saved Miro from Powerhouse Hobbs.

She then cut a promo on Miro for rejecting her at AEW All Out. The former Ravishing Rusian took to her Instagram and uploaded a gorgeous photo of herself wearing underwear while hyping up her appearance on AEW Collision.

Heading to @aew #AEWCollision tonight from fashion week. Don’t miss me addressing what’s going on with @tobemiro tonight on @tntdrama 8/7c
CJPerry.com

CJ Perry also has not signed long-term deal with AEW following her debut. We’ll have to wait and see what CJ Perry will do on tonight’s episode of AEW Collision.
