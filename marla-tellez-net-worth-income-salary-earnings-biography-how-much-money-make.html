Marla Tellez is a Los Angeles, California-based news anchor for KTTV-TV. She attended Sonoma State University and majored in political science. Tellez worked as an anchor for KNTV news, a local NBC affiliate in San Francisco, before moving to Los Angeles. She had previously worked as a producer and reporter for CBS in San Francisco, as well as an anchor for KOB in Albuquerque, New Mexico. She was a news presenter in an episode of the popular programme Breaking Bad during her time in New Mexico. In January 2014, Tellez began working as a news anchor on Good Day L.A., a local Fox morning show in Los Angeles, California.
