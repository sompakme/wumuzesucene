Marcus Holman (born May 2, 1991 in Baltimore, Maryland, United States) is an Ohio Machine professional lacrosse player. Holman was an attackman for the University of North Carolina Tar Heels, starting 52 of UNC’s 62 games in the previous four years (84 percent). He is the all-time top scorer at UNC. Brian Holman, Holman’s father, instructs UNC’s goalkeepers, operates the replacement box, and assists with defensive coordination. In his first year at Chapel Hill, he joined head coach Joe Breschi. Matthew, Marcus’ elder brother, was the Tar Heels’ goalie in 2011 and 201His mother works for the North Carolina Tar Heels women’s lacrosse team, which his sister Sydney wants to join for the 2014 season.
