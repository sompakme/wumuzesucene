Lady Adorns Tight Edo Traditional Attire, Looks Uncomfortable, Netizens Laugh at Her: &quot;Try Breathe&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-23
  • A lady got the attention of netizens after she adorned a tight Edo traditional attire that made breathing difficult for her
  • She said her friend asked her to help model the attire, and though it didn't fit, she still had to manage it
  • Some netizens thought she was a bride who was simply focused on snatching her waist, and they laughed at her

A lady, Nicky Charles, has gotten netizens talking after she adorned an attire that looked uncomfortable.

She wore an Edo traditional attire. She combined the red outfit with okuku (Benin hairstyle). To add glamour to her attire, she wore coral beads on each wrist and neck.

The outfit seemed to be choking her as she was finding it difficult to breathe properly. She looked so uncomfortable, making netizens wonder why she stressed herself to adorn the attire.
