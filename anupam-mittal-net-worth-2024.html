Early Life and Education

Anupam Mittal was born and raised in India. He completed his education from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay. His entrepreneurial journey began right after his graduation when he started his first venture, People Group.

Establishment of People Group

People Group was established in 1997 with a vision to connect people and create life-changing opportunities. The group’s first venture, Shaadi.com, revolutionized the concept of arranged marriages in India and became a leading matrimonial website.

Expansion of People Group

After the success of Shaadi.com, People Group expanded its portfolio by launching Makaan.com, a real estate portal, and Mauj Mobile, a mobile media company. These ventures further increased Mittal’s wealth and established him as a successful entrepreneur.
