How do I adjust the force on my Genie garage door opener?

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Answer

Located to the right of the travel limit and set buttons on the power head unit control panel, the “Open Force” and “Close Force” controls are used to open and close the force. Shift the “Close Force” control to the left to reduce the amount of force applied; turn the control to the right to increase the amount of force applied.

So, how do you programme a genie Mach Force garage door opener, to give you an example?

Release the button on the remote that you wish to configure after pressing and holding it; the LED indication will flash or light consistently (varies by model). The LED indication will be turned off if you press the same remote button again. To ensure that the button has been programmed successfully, push the button on the remote control a second time; the door should now open.

How can I change the tension on my garage door opener, in a similar manner?

The outside nut (the one nearest to the garage door) should be slack enough to allow for the chain drooping below the base of the rail. The inner nut should also be slightly loose enough to enable space for the chain to be tightened. Tightening the outer nut will increase the tension in the chain. With each turn of the outer nut, the chain will ascend a fraction of an inch.
