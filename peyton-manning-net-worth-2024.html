Career Earnings from the NFL

Peyton Manning’s career in the NFL spanned 18 seasons, during which he established himself as one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time. His earnings from contracts with the Indianapolis Colts and the Denver Broncos form a significant portion of his wealth. Manning’s salary, bonuses, and other incentives contributed to his substantial financial gains from his playing days.

Indianapolis Colts Compensation

During his tenure with the Colts, Manning became the highest-paid player in the NFL at various points. His contracts, including a five-year, $90 million deal signed in 2011, set the bar for quarterback salaries at the time.

Denver Broncos Earnings

After joining the Denver Broncos, Manning continued to command top dollar, earning millions per season and leading the team to a Super Bowl victory, which further enhanced his marketability and earning potential.
