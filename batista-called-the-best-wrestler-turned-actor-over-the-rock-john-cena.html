Batista Called The Best Wrestler Turned Actor Over The Rock &amp; John Cena

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Dave Bautista is a sought-after talent in the entertainment world, having made a name for himself as Drax the Destroyer in the popular Marvel’s Guardians of the Galaxy film franchise. This former WWE wrestler has successfully transitioned into acting, demonstrating his versatility and commitment to the craft. Now it appears that even Bronson Reed has a very high opinion of Batista as an actor.

Before landing the role of Drax in Marvel’s Guardians Of The Galaxy, Bautista revealed he was struggling financially due to tax issues. But his portrayal of Drax launched his career, leading to prominent roles in Blade Runner 2049, Dune and many other upcoming projects.

Apart from Batista, both The Rock and John Cena are also professional wrestlers who became major stars in Hollywood thanks to their portrayal in numerous movies. That said, everyone has their favorite among the three.

While speaking on Out Of Character with Ryan Satin, Bronson Reed stated that Batista is his top “wrestler-turned-actor” pick, when asked to choose between Batista, The Rock and John Cena.

“I’m a big of Batista and his acting work. I really liked the Knock at the Cabin that came out this year as well. I think I am gonna go out and be controversial and get some heat for this. But I think he is the best wrestler turned actor there is.”

Dave Bautista’s acting career is thriving, as he’s expressed a desire to take on diverse roles. It remains to be seen what other parts he has planned, but fans eagerly anticipate his future performances.
