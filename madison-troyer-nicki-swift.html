Expertise

The Royal Family, Farmer Wants A Wife, '90s Teen Stars

  • Born and raised in Idaho and having spent her entire adult life in NYC, Madison was thrilled to hear the dating show "Farmer Wants a Wife" was getting a reboot. She's seen every episode multiple times, and stalks her favorite contestants' social media pages regularly.
  • Madison is obsessed with royals of all stripes, and particularly loves to follow royal wedding coverage, be it that of Harry and Meghan or Princess Victoria and Daniel.
  • A true child of the '90s, Madison still has some of her favorite Teen Beat tear-out posters adorning the walls of her NYC apartment (though these days they're framed and professionally hung, not tacked to the back of the door with scotch tape).

Experience

Madison has been covering pop culture and celebrities for the better part of a decade. Her work has been published by several online publications including Stacker, Newsweek, MSN, and TheThings.com. Since joining Static Media in 2021, she's loved taking readers on deep dives into celebrity culture and lifestyles.

Education

Madison graduated from The King's College in 2015 with a B.A. in Media, Culture, and the Arts.
