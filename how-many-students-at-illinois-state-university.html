So, You Wanna Be a Redbird? Decoding the Illinois State University Enrollment Numbers (and Maybe Avoiding a Few Birds Along the Way)

Ever wondered how many classmates you'll be dodging on the quad at Illinois State University (ISU)? Well, buckle up, future Redbird, because we're about to dive into the fascinating world of ISU enrollment statistics... with a touch of feathery fun!

Birds of a Feather: The Overall Flock

As of fall 2023, ISU boasts a population of over 20,683 students. That's a lot of Redbird pride (and maybe a few rogue pigeons)! This includes both undergraduate birds (freshmen to seniors) clocking in at around 18,055 and graduate students soaring towards their degrees at roughly 2,628.

Where'd They All Come From? The Global Nest

ISU isn't just for Illinois natives! The flock is delightfully diverse, with over 1,000 students migrating from 47 states and a whopping 74 countries. So, if you're worried about being outnumbered by cornfields, fear not! You might just find yourself sharing a dorm room with an international birdbrain (and that's a good thing!).

Feathered Friends on a Budget: The Financial Aid Flock

Let's face it, college can be expensive. But fear not, future Redbird! ISU is committed to making your education affordable. Four out of five students receive some form of financial aid, so you won't be left singing the financial blues (unless you're a music major, that is).

How to Avoid Getting Lost in the Flock: A Quick Guide

Okay, so with over 20,000 students, campus might seem a little overwhelming. But don't worry, there are ways to navigate the Redbird flock with ease:

  • Get to know your resident advisor (RA): They're basically your on-campus GPS and feathered friend rolled into one.
  • Download the ISU app: It has maps, schedules, and everything you need to avoid becoming a campus pigeon.
  • Join a club or organization: There's a club for practically everything at ISU, so you're bound to find your flock with similar interests.

Feathered Frequently Asked Questions (Flock FAQs):

How to Apply to ISU?

Head over to the ISU admissions website and get ready to spread your wings!

How Much Does it Cost to Attend ISU?

Costs vary depending on residency and program, but you can find a detailed breakdown on the ISU financial aid website.

How Competitive is Getting into ISU?

The acceptance rate is around 86%, so put your best foot (or wing) forward in your application!

How much Housing Does ISU Offer?

ISU offers a variety of on-campus housing options, so you're sure to find a nest that suits you.

How Many Birds Does ISU Actually Have?

Thankfully, not that many! But keep an eye out for pigeons – they're the real campus hustlers.

So there you have it, future Redbird! You're well on your way to joining the thriving flock at Illinois State University. Now get out there, spread your wings, and make the most of your Redbird experience (just avoid those pigeons)!
