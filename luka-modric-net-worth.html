Luka Modric is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Real Madrid and the Croatian national team. Born on September 9, 1985 in Zadar, Croatia, Modric joined Real Madrid in 2012 after spending four seasons with Tottenham Hotspur. He made his debut for the Croatian national team in 2006 and has since become an integral part of the team.
