WWE Payback Complete Spoiler Line-Up &amp; Planned Match Times

Publish date: 2024-08-21

WWE hasn’t brought a pay-per-view to Pittsburgh in a while, and Payback was worth the wait. Now we know what the order of the show will be, so that should be your spoiler warning if you like to be surprised.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE Payback.

Sean Sapp noted the match order for Tonight’s WWE Payback show. He also noted the producers for each match and segment during the night next to them on the list.

– Cage match: Becky Lynch vs. Trish Stratus (producer Petey Williams)

–  John Cena promo

– LA Knight vs. The Miz (producer Shane Helms)

– United States Title: Rey Mysterio (c) vs. Austin Theory (producer Adam Pearce)

– Tag Team Titles: Kevin Owens & Sami Zayn (c) vs. Judgment Day (producer Michael Hayes)

– Grayson Waller Effect with Cody Rhodes (producer Shawn Daivari)

– Women’s World Title: Rhea Ripley (c) vs. Raquel Rodriguez (producer Jason Jordan)

– World Heavyweight Title: Seth Rollins (c) vs. Shinsuke Nakamura (producer Jamie Noble)

It was also noted that, “Nick Aldis and Bobby Roode are not listed for producer assignments tonight.” Also, there was a lot more, as the report noted the tentative times that each match is getting.

“Tentative times: 10 minutes- Cena promo, Grayson Waller Effect, 15 minutes- Knight/Miz, Mysterio/Theory. 18 minutes- Tag titles, women’s title, Becky/Trish. 20 minutes- Rollins vs. Nakamura”
