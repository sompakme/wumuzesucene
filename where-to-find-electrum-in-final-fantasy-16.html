Where To Find Electrum In Final Fantasy 16

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Want to know where to find Electrum in Final Fantasy 16? Look no further than this step-by-step guide to get this crafting material in FFXVI.

First Published June 24, 2023, 08:14

Are you wondering where to find Electrum in Final Fantasy 16? We get it. As one of the rarer crafting materials in FF16, it's only natural that many players might want to find it. This is especially true given that Electrum is one of the components required for crafting Drakeslayer armor pieces.

However, the burning question remains: How to get to the Electrum location in Final Fantasy XVI? But don't worry; we'll be your guiding beacon to uncover this elusive and supposedly naturally occurring material. So, without further ado, let's get started.

TL;DR: You can get Electrum by beating Severian in Final Fantasy 16. The B-Rank monster is located in the northern part of Sorrowise in Rosaria, accessible via the Martha's Rest Obelisk. You're welcome.

How To Get Electrum In Final Fantasy XVI (FF16)

You can get Electrum in Final Fantasy 16 by defeating Severian, the Notorious Mark. That's because, as the item's in-game description suggests, the crafting material is sourced from the remains of Fallen automata. That being said, the next question is, where is Severian's location in FFXVI?

Valisthean prospectors have yet to locate a source of naturally occurring electrum, the only known samples coming from the remains of Fallen automata. This has led some to surmise that [Electrum] is not, in fact, naturally occurring at all, and rather a creation of the lost people.

Where To Find Electrum Location In Final Fantasy XVI

Severian is located in the northern part of Sorrowise in the Imperial Province of Rosaria. To get there, Fast Travel to the Martha's Rest Obelisk and navigate west into Sorrowise. The image above pinpoints the beast's location. You will receive Electrum as a reward for slaying Severian in Final Fantasy 16. 

If you're struggling to reach Severian from the Obelisk, follow these instructions: Take the lift down at the settlement and immediately go west. Then, take the first turn right and continue ahead. You'll eventually find a Severian on the ground. Interact with the mount to trigger the battle.

For the sake of conciseness, we won't go into detail on how to beat Severian in Final Fantasy XVI, as he shouldn't pose any significant threat. However, you would do well to keep your distance and parry his spinning and projectile attacks. Note that this beast is quite mobile, so equip fast-casting Eikons.

And that's everything you need to know about where to find Electrum in Final Fantasy 16. While you're at it, you might as well learn how to find Griffin, too.
