Who was Adari Tulasi Rao? Wiki, Biography, Age, Wife, Family &amp; Facts About Visakha Dairy Chairman

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Adari Tulasi Rao (born on February 1, 1939, age: 84 years when died) was a businessman and chairman of Sri Vijaya Milk Producers Company Limited also known as Visakha Dairy in India. He joined Visakha Dairy between 1985-1986 and till today he was working for this company.

He was honored as the chairperson of Visakha Dairy. He took Visakha Diary to new heights and increased its turnover to 1400 crore in 2022. But this great man whose efforts gave new life to many people is now more among us.

How did Adari Tulasi Rao die?

Adari Tulasi Rao died on 04th January 2023. His death news was shared by his kids and wife in the media. He passed away in Hyderabad where he was admitted to a private hospital. Tulasi Rao was having treatment for an illness that he was facing for a long. But sadly his health did not recover and he died.

After his death, his dead body was sent to Yelamanchili in the Anakapalli district where it was kept for his lovers to pay last tribute to the businessman. Chief Minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy met Adari’s son Adari Anand Kumar to pay homage to the Adari Tulasi.

Adari Tulasi Rao’s Biography

Adari Tulasi Rao was born in Hyderabad on February 1, 1939. He had his education till 10th Grade and after it got involved in agriculture and animal welfare work. He spent his early life learning new things about agriculture and poultry farming. When he became a young man he began participating in political matters. He remained a part of Janata Party1, Telugu Desma Party, and Swatanta.

Career

Sharing the successful journey of Adari Tulasi Rao then when he was 23 years old young boy he got the honors to become a director of a Cooperative urban bank in 1962.

He later became a Sarpanch of Yelamanchili. He continued working as Sarpanch for the next 25 years. Then in 1986, he was elected as the Chairman of Visakha Dairy founded in 1977.

 I promise all our milk producers that I will always try my best to provide maximum benefits to milk producers by way of providing them the maximum price of their Milk and satisfy our consumers with highest quality of products at competitive rates.

When he became Chairman Visakha Dairy’s turnover was 11 Crore INR per year. And now before its death, he brought net worth to 1400 Crore INR.

He not only increased the Turnover of the company but increased its milk production to 10 lakh liters per day from 50000 liters per day.

Adari Tulasi Rao expanded Visakha Dairy to other states such as West Bengal, Odisha, and Madhya Pradesh. He was the one who converted Visakha Dairy into a registered company in 2006 with the name Sri Vijaya Milk Producers Company Limited.

He listed his name as the first person to bring the ultra-pasteurized milk technology to India from Finland Elecster which helps the milk to stay fresh for the next 14 days.

He along with his son Adari Anand Kumar and a family member Yuvaraj Adari founded a real estate company called Adari Tulasi Rao Estates Private Limited on 30th July 2014 in Vijaywada.

This company’s authorized share capital was 15,000,000 INR and its paid-up capital was 5,000,000 INR in 2014. Whereas his company Sri Vijaya Visakha Milk Producers Company Limited’s authorized share capital was 2,500,000,000 INR and its paid-up capital is 2,129,403,520 INR when it was registered in 2006.
