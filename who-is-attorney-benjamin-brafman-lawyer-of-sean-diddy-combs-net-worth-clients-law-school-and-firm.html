Benjamin Brafman is a famous American lawyer known for helping people in serious trouble. One of his big cases is with Sean “Diddy” Combs, the music boss, facing serious accusations about things like rape and forcing someone into the sex trade.

Strategy in Defense

The person accusing Combs, Casandra Ventura, says she suffered for a long time. She says Combs hurt her physically and sexually when she was just 19, and he was way older. The case talks about harsh beatings leaving marks like blood, bruises, and black eyes.

Denial and Fight Back

Combs says Ventura’s claims are all lies, a trick to get money from him. His lawyer, Benjamin Brafman, strongly disagrees. He says these accusations are terrible and completely untrue.

Claims of Tricks

The defense says Ventura wanted $30 million to keep quiet about things between her and Combs. Brafman says that’s just blackmail, nothing else.

Big Names Brafman’s Helped

Benjamin Brafman is known for helping many famous people in court. He’s worked for Dominique Strauss-Kahn, who was a big shot at the International Monetary Fund, rapper Jay-Z, and NFL star Plaxico Burress.

Growing Up and Learning

Born on July 21, 1948, in New York City, Brafman’s family survived the Holocaust. He went to Yeshiva High School and then studied at Brooklyn College and Ohio Northern University College of Law.

Life Beyond Law

Apart from his job, Brafman is happily married to Lynda Brafman and has two kids, David and Jennifer. At 75 years old, he’s worth $10 million.

The Final Verdict

Benjamin Brafman is famous for being really good at defending people in serious trouble. His way of helping clients in big cases has made him a big name in the legal world.
