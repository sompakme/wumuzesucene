Ron Kamonohashis Forbidden Deductions Season 2:- Ron Kamonohashi’s Forbidden Deductions is a mystery anime series based on a manga by Akira Amano, the creator of Katekyo Hitman Reborn and the character designer of Psycho-Pass. The anime follows the adventures of Ron Kamonohashi, a genius detective who was expelled from the top detective academy and became a recluse, and Totomaru Isshiki, a rookie police officer who seeks Ron’s help in solving various cases. Together, they form an unlikely duo of detectives who use their wits and skills to crack the most baffling mysteries.

The first season of the anime premiered on October 2, 2023 and ran for 13 episodes. It received positive reviews from fans and critics, who praised its intriguing plot, quirky characters, and humorous tone. The anime also featured a catchy opening theme song by LiSA and a soothing ending theme song by Yui Horie.
