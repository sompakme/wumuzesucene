Watch Episode 3 of 21 Savage's &quot;The Year 2100&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Who can really say what the moral of "The Year 2100" is going to be, or if there even will be one in the end. Nonetheless in this week's 1-minute episode the story continues to develop.

We find super-villain Savage on the streets, fending off half-limbed cyborgs with different tracks of his. However it seems he's getting weaker, and his tracks less and less effective, as his sidekick will note. He also takes the time to visit his hometown after stopping by the "Hypebeast Forest," a place where Yeezy's and more, grow abundantly. When he does finally arrive in "Neo-Atlanta," the population is astonishingly zero!
