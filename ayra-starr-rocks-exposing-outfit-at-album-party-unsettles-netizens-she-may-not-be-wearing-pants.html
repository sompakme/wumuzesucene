Ayra Starr Rocks Exposing Outfit at Album Party, Unsettles Netizens: &quot;She May Not Be Wearing Pants&quot;

Publish date: 2024-07-16
  • Nigerians have not come to terms with the idea of singer Ayra Starr rocking scanty clothes at events
  • She wore yet another provocative outfit to her album listening party that made fans tackle her, describing the dress as tacky
  • While some people made comments about her sitting position, others wondered if she was wearing underwear

Some social media users have expressed disgust at the rate at which singer Oyinkansola Aderibigbe, aka Ayra Starr, goes out scantily dressed.

At her latest album listening party, she rocked a blue top and mini-skirt that exposed her thighs as she sat. She still left little to the imagination though she tried to adjust it and cover up a bit.

The title of her album is The Year I Turned 21, otherwise called ‘TYIT21’, and will be released on May 31, 2024.
