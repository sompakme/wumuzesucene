Actress Lilian Afegbai shares beautiful new photos as she turns 28

Publish date: 2024-08-31

- Nigerian actress, Lilian Afegbai, is celebrating a new age

- The film star clocked 28 on November 11, 2019

- To celebrate the occasion, Lilian shared stunning photos of herself in different outfits

The Nigerian entertainment industry plays host to a number of talented entertainers who have also managed to have the hearts of a number of fans.

Many times, these celebs are able to show their fans love on their birthdays or through social media interactions.

For many celebs in the country, it has become a tradition to release lovely photos of themselves on their birthdays to the joy of fans. In the case of actress Lilian Afegbai, it was not any different.

The beautiful thespian who clocked 28 on November 11, 2019, shared lovely photos of herself on her social media page.

In some of the photos, she dressed in a simple unbuttoned white shirt tied at the waist, paired with blue jeans.
