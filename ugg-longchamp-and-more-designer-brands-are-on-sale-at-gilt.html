Ugg, Longchamp, and More Designer Brands Are on Sale at Gilt

Publish date: 2024-07-03

When I tell people I shop for a living, they think it’s a cheeky joke to cover up my shopaholic tendencies. Well, it’s not. As a shopping editor, it’s literally my job to scour the internet for the best deals, stay on top of trends, and (naturally) secure a piece or two for myself. Afterall, if I didn't try out the products, how would I know if they were any good or not? But in all seriousness, I’m a master at scouting out savings opportunities, and one of those big-time deals events is going on right now at Gilt.

If you’ve never heard of Gilt before, or simply need a refresher, it’s a members-only sale site that offers major savings on coveted labels — think Birkenstock, Longchamp, Ugg, and more. Accessing the deals isn’t hard; all you have to do is sign up for a free account with your email address. (Easy peasy!) And if I were you, I’d do so sooner rather than later, because Gilt is offering tons of early Black Friday deals right now for up to 68 percent off. Seriously, prices are so low, even the denim brand Oprah wears starts at $38. But items are selling out fast, so add them to your cart before somebody else swoops them up and they sell out.

Longchamp Le Pliage Large Nylon Tote

Kate Middleton’s go-to Longchamp Le Pliage nylon tote bag is 22 percent off right now, bringing the iconic accessory down to just $120. The roomy tote is accentuated with gold-toned details, has a top handle, and is made complete with a subtle logo on the upper closure — honestly, it’s everything and more. The inside is just as exciting, including two functional pockets and a spacious interior.   

I’ve not only seen this bag strung on the arms of royalty, but also all over New York City. It’s the ‘It’ bag to have, whether you’re headed to work, school, or brunch — and it’s totally worth the on-sale price.

Stuart Weitzman Siella Suede Knee-High Boots

I personally have heart eyes for these Stuart Weitzman knee-high boots that are 52 percent off. It seems like everyone from Amal Clooney to Taylor Swift is donning the sexy silhouette, making me love this discounted pick even more. The boots feature sleek black suede and stretchy material, as well as lightly padded footbeds. And don’t even get me started on the gold-toned hardware. The buckle-like accent takes these boots to the next level, elevating just about any outfit you wear them with.

French Connection Katrin Dress

I firmly believe in the power of knitted dresses during the fall. They’re chic, comfortable, and so easy to style. That’s why I was ecstatic to see that French Conenction’s Katrin dress was marked down by 53 percent. The now $60 dress embodies a modern elegance, boasting a cable-knit design and mock neck. Style it with transparents tights and a pair of knee-high boots (like the above Stuart Weitzman pair, wink wink), and you’ve got the ultimate fall look. If you’re feeling extra adventurous, throw on a red cardigan for some contrast.      

Nautica Puffer Jacket

Whether you’re ready to accept it or not, the temperatures are quickly dropping, and you’re going to want to be prepared for when the ice-cold chill hits. Thankfully, Nautica has one of the most elegant puffer jackets I’ve seen this season, featuring a slimming fit, chevron design, and cloud-white color. The removable hood with faux fur trim has to be one of the greatest features, while the mock collar and front pockets trail close behind.
